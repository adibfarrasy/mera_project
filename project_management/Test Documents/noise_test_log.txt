Device Temperature Measurement by Adibaby
[17/08/2020]

Procedure:
1. turn on the device.
2. measure noise with app for every minute 1m away from the device.
3. terminate test when noise is higher than 62dB. device fails the test.
4. if average noise per minute is lower than 62dB for 5 minutes, device passes the test.
